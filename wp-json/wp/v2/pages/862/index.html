CyberEvangelist:<\/p>\n

Assist the worldwide Lead CyberEvangelist in oversight of a cross-functional volunteer team of Audio Engineers, Social Media Specialists, Web Developers, Graphic Artists, and Programmers to develop enterprise-level business solutions, aligning the goals of 147 churches with the overall worldwide initiatives in 58 nations. Develop global security and business continuity plans to ensure high availability of all media. Centralize media assets and social media accounts to create a cohesive and collaborative family of churches operating as one unified, worldwide enterprise unit. Develop mediums for presenting spiritual media (Video, Audio, eBooks) to a worldwide audience in 14 languages. Develop ADA-accessible captions for the deaf and hard of hearing.<\/p>\n

Administrator:<\/p>\n

Manage a team of volunteer bookkeepers with direct oversight of the DC Church finances. Develop standardized procedures and corresponding systems for membership management, worldwide event management\/registration, and tracking of weekly\/ad-hoc contributions and attendances.
\nKey Achievements:
\n– Created a duplicatable model for tracking church finances and membership.
